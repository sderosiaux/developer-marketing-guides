## 3. Developer Marketing Strategies That Work

Based on Kelsey Hightower's X discussion, three core principles emerged:

**The Winning Formula:**
- "Don't try to impress us, try to help us"
- "Don't make it feel like marketing"
- "Let us get our hands dirty (with your product)"

**Developer Preferences:**
- Practical value over polish
- Authentic education over corporate messaging
- Hands-on product experience matters most

**Core Insight:** Developers respond to transparency and utility, rejecting overtly promotional tactics in favor of genuine problem-solving assistance.
