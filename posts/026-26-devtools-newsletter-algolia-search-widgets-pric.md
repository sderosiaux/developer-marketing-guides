## 2. Algolia Search Widgets

Algolia executed a textbook product-led growth strategy through free search widgets.

**Their approach:**
- Distributed free search boxes with minimal restrictions
- Targeted sites where their core audience congregated
- Added "Powered by Algolia" branding on free versions
- High-visibility placements included Hacker News search, FontAwesome, open-source documentation sites, and SteamDB

**The win-win-win structure:**
Users gained superior search functionality, host sites improved their platforms, and Algolia converted curious developers into paying customers—aligning all incentives simultaneously.

## 3. Retool Pricing Page Copy

Rather than ambiguous deployment terminology, Retool simplified their hosting options:
- "Cloud (we host)"
- "Self-hosted (you host)"

This straightforward language eliminates buyer confusion about infrastructure flexibility and scalability assumptions.
